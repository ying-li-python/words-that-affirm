Who Am I


I am a self-starter who is passionate about using technology for social good. At the office, at the home, or at the lab, I am curious about the technology that have inspired us to get here. With every data point I plot, I think about how it can be translated into a meaningful story.

Read my resume





How I started

I earned a PhD in Entomology and gained industry experience as a researcher at Genentech. I am also a proud graduate of the Data Analytics Bootcamp at UC Berkeley Extension.








Where Am I

Currently, I am a Data Engineer at SymphonyRM in Palo Alto, CA. In addition to data, I spend my time as a web designer for grassroots organizations in SF Bay Area.
